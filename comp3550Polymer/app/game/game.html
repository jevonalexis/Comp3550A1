IT TAKES A LOT OF BLUE TO GO GREEN

On a planet covered with 70% water, beings composed of 60% water, still manage to waste this precious substance.
Your mission, should you choose to accept it,is to save this neglected planet
from a shortage that will propagate our own impending doom.
It is up to you. Save the Earth. Save Mankind. Save the WATER!

Instructions:

      Drip. Drip. Drop. Save the Water. Watch Out For The Clock.
      Use the arrow keys to move the Bucket of Preservation Left and Right.
      Collect the water drops from the Earth Pipe as they drip slowly towards shortage.
      But don't forget time is upon us, keep an eye on your CLOCK.

You've taken the first step towards averting this crisis.
Now go spread the word. Tell a friend. Help someone else save water.
